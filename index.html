Nairobi City

The capital city

The city is popularly referred to as the Green City in the Sun. Nairobi is the is the only capital city with a national park. It is the largest city in Kenya. It was founded in 1907.

Nakuru

The Metropolitan Town.

The third largest urban area in Kenya.It is the capital of Nakuru County and formerly of Rift-Valley Province. It is found in the Rift-Valley and host L.Nakuru a major tourist attraction.

Mombasa

Coastal city

Coastal city of Kenya along the Indian Ocean.It is Kenya's oldest and second largest in the country. It is a tourism based city. Swahili tends to be the most spoken language.

Kisumu

The Lake city

The third largest city in Kenya.It serves as the headquarter of the Lake Region Economic Bloc, a conglomeratio of 14countries. it is the capital center Kisumu county. It is widely famous for being the hometown of Barack Obama.
